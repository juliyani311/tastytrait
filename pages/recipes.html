Choco Chip Chocolate


Prep time : 15 minutes
Cook time : 15 minutes
Serves : 20 Cookies


Chocolate cookies are one of the favorite snacks for friends to chat with or friends to drink coffee, tea or warm milk with. The recipe and how to make it are also very easy because the ingredients are easy to get. Apart from chocochips, you can add other mixed ingredients such as sliced almonds or other types of nuts. INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a medium sized mixing bowl combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt with a whisk. Whisk until you can no longer see white.
  2. In a mixer cream butter and sugars. Add in yogurt and mix just until combined.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix on medium speed until fluffy.
  4. With the mixer on low, slowly add the flour mixture a little at a time. Mix until combined.
  5. Slowly add in the chocolate chips, reserving Â½ a cup of chips.
  6. Refrigerate dough for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line two cookie sheets with parchment or silpats.
  8. Evenly drop cookie dough using a tablespoon scoop onto cookie sheet. Place a few of the reserved chocolate chips onto each cookie prior to baking.
  9. Bake 8-10 minutes.
